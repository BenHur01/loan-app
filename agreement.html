Loan Agreement Terms

IsandoTom Finances & Borrower

1. Parties involved:

This agreement is entered into between IsandoTom Finances (The Lender) and the Borrower (Applicant).

2. Loan Amount:

The Borrower requests a principal loan amount (between R200 and R2000). The final approved amount will be confirmed upon successful application review.

3. Interest and Repayment:

The loan carries an interest rate of 0.50% (monthly). The repayment period is 30 days from the date of disbursement.

4. Default:

Failure to repay the loan amount plus interest within the 30-day period will result in penalties and legal action.

5. Requirements:

The Borrower must provide a valid ID, proof of residence, and bank statement. All information must be truthful.

6. Consent:

By proceeding, the Borrower acknowledges and agrees to these terms.
